Arroz Con Dulce

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Rice
  • 5 cups Coconut Milk, reserve 3/4 cup for later
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 oz Fresh Ginger, cut into chunks
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 6 Cloves, whole
  • Pinch of Nutmeg
  • Raisins to taste

Directions

  1. Cover rice with water and let soak for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Combine the coconut milk, salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg in a medium sized caldero. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a medium heat and boil for 15 minutes, without stirring.
  3. Drain the rice and add it to the boiling mixture. Mix and reduce heat to low, simmering until the rice is dry.
  4. Add the reserved coconut milk, sugar, and raisins. Simmer over medium heat for another 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally to keep from sticking to the bottom.
