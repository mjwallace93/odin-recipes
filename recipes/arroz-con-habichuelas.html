Arroz Con Habichuelas

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Chicken Broth or Water
  • Bacon
  • 1 cup Sofrito
  • 2 cans Pink Beans
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1 packet Sazon
  • Olives
  • Capers

Directions

  1. Cook the bacon, then add sofrito and sazon.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, olives, beans, broth and capers. Simmer for about an hour or until done.
  3. Cook the rice separately and serve topped with the habichuelas.
