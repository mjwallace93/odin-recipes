Spiced Pear Simple Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 1 1/2 cups Commice or Anjou Pears, Any kind will work
  • 3/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3 Cinnamon Sticks

Directions

  1. Add the water, pears, sugar, and cinnamon sticks to a large sauce pan over medium heat. Stir gently until the sugar has completely dissolved.
  2. Gently simmer for about 30 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat and allow to cool for 15 minutes.
  4. Strain the syrup into a glass measuring cup and discard the pears and cinnamon sticks. I like to save the pears as a topping for ice cream.
  5. Strain the syrup a second time into a glass bottle or container that can be tightly sealed for storage.
  6. Refrigerate for up to two weeks.
