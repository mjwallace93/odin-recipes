Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Minced Garlic
  • Seasonings to taste
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. In a large frying pan add the Olive Oil and Garlic, sauté over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the Spinach and cook until Spinach is wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Butterfly the chicken breasts and lay them out flat on the cutting board.
  5. Place a slice or two of Provolone cheese over each chicken breast. Add Spinach and garlic on top of the cheese.
  6. Fold the chicken in half over the stuffing, being careful to keep the filling from spilling out.
  7. Place the breasts on a greased baking dish and season the chicken however you like. Bake uncovered for 30 Minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165 F.
